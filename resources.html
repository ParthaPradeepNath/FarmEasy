Frequently Asked Questions

How does the flood alert system work?

Our flood alert system uses a combination of IoT sensors, satellite data, and machine learning algorithms to predict and monitor potential flood risks in real-time. The system collects data from multiple sources including:

  • Ground-based water level sensors
  • Weather stations and rainfall gauges
  • Satellite imagery for terrain analysis
  • Historical flood data and patterns

This data is processed through our advanced AI algorithms to provide accurate and timely flood predictions.

What types of sensors are used in the system?

We use various types of sensors including:

  • Water level sensors for rivers and water bodies
  • Soil moisture sensors at different depths
  • Automated rainfall gauges
  • Weather stations for atmospheric data
  • Flow meters for water movement

These sensors work together to provide comprehensive environmental monitoring and early warning capabilities.

Can I integrate the system with my existing farm management tools?

Yes, our system provides comprehensive API integration capabilities that allow it to work seamlessly with most modern farm management systems and IoT platforms. We support:

  • REST API integration
  • Real-time data streaming
  • Custom data export formats
  • Third-party software plugins

Our team can help you set up the integration with your existing systems.

What is the coverage area of the flood alert system?

The system can cover agricultural areas of any size, from small farms to large agricultural regions. Our coverage includes:

  • Individual farm plots (minimum 1 acre)
  • Large agricultural estates
  • Regional watershed areas
  • River basins and floodplains

Coverage is customized based on your specific needs and local geographical conditions.

How often is the data updated?

Our system provides frequent updates across different data types:

  • Sensor readings: Every 5-15 minutes
  • Satellite data: Every 6-12 hours
  • Weather forecasts: Hourly updates
  • Risk assessments: Real-time calculations

Update frequencies can be customized based on your specific monitoring needs and local conditions.
